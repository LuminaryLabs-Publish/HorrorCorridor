## Player-View Intent

- Foreground: wet broken paving, mud, rubble, roots, and floor/wall separation.
- Midground: warm lamp, rusted door, and generator form the primary route landmark.
- Background: restrained anomaly pedestal and broken-ceiling moonlight pull the player deeper.
- Navigation: one legible path through a tight room, with dressing kept outside the walkable lane.
- Mood: darkness shapes attention while every important layer remains readable.
