# HorrorCorridor Natural Domain Composition

Status: completed

## Objective

Replace the mechanical HorrorCorridor taxonomy with a natural composition whose domains own coherent state and meaning and whose behaviors, bridges, platform adapters, and proofs remain explicitly separate.

## Public Sources

- `NexusEngine/docs/ideal/domains.md`
- `NexusEngine/docs/ideal/kits.md`
- `NexusEngine/docs/ideal/services.md`
- `NexusEngine/docs/ideal/composition.md`

## Requirements

- The composition covers the expedition, corridor, party, anomaly objective, dread, and cooperative session.
- Domain names use natural subject language rather than architecture labels.
- Each nested domain stays inside its parent and owns an independently meaningful truth.
- Domain leaves stop naturally; they do not need forced atomic-kit descendants.
- Every domain mounts at least one concise behavior-oriented kit without turning that kit into another domain.
- Kits, services, hosts, and proofs appear inline at their actual composition owner with explicit role labels.
- The deep tree example retains biological depth where real nested ownership exists, especially tree, branch, twig, bud, and leaf life.

- 6 natural top-level domains
- 107 total domain boundaries
- 327 owned state truths
- 160 unique kit contracts, with at least one mounted directly on every domain
- 6 controlled services, 4 platform hosts, and 4 validation proofs
- 16-level maximum depth along the natural tree-to-leaf lineage
- No skipped levels or state nodes with children
- No domain names shaped as systems, kits, services, managers, modules, layers, adapters, proofs, renderers, or algorithms
- ASCII and whitespace checks pass
